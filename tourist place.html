paris


Paris is the capital and largest city of France. It is one of the world's most famous tourist destinations, known for its history, art, fashion, and culture.
The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, the Arc de Triomphe, and the Notre-Dame Cathedral.
The Seine River flows through the center of Paris, adding to its scenic beauty.
Paris is also known for its cafés, delicious French cuisine, luxury shopping, and important role in art and literature.
Millions of visitors travel to Paris every year to experience its rich heritage and vibrant atmosphere.
April to June (Spring) – Best overall time. The weather is pleasant (10–20°C), flowers are in bloom, parks are beautiful, and it's ideal for sightseeing.

September to October (Autumn) – Another excellent time. The weather is cool and comfortable, with fewer crowds than summer and beautiful autumn colors.

July to August (Summer) – Warm (20–30°C) with long daylight hours. This is the busiest tourist season, so attractions are crowded and hotels are more expensive.

November to February (Winter) – Cold (3–10°C) but less crowded. Christmas decorations and festive markets make December especially charming.
